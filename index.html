Game Mode

A.I. Vs Player

In a game of chess between an AI and a human player, the AI would have a significant advantage. The AI would be able to analyze the board and calculate all possible moves and their consequences much faster than a human player could. The AI would also be able to use its vast database of past games and strategies to inform its decisions.

Player vs Player

During the game, both players have the opportunity to analyze the board and calculate possible moves and their consequences. They can also anticipate their opponent's moves and plan accordingly. Each player must carefully consider their options and make strategic decisions that will put them in the best possible position.
